About

We all love those spunky Minions and their non-stop chatter, but what in the world are they saying? The Minions have their own language called Minionese!

This app will convert your english language to minion or banana language.the Minions language is mostly unintelligible or barely intelligible talking with some words from the Spanish, French, Italian, Filipino, and other foreign languages
